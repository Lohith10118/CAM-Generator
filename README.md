# Intelli-Credit AI

AI-powered Credit Decisioning Engine for automated Credit Appraisal Memo (CAM) generation.

## Problem
Corporate credit appraisal is slow and fragmented due to multi-source structured and unstructured data.

## Solution
Our AI engine:
- Extracts financial data from PDFs
- Reconciles GST and Bank flows
- Performs web-scale secondary research
- Calculates explainable Five-Cs risk score
- Generates professional CAM document
